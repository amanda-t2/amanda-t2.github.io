    TIME SERIES ANALYSIS

    For this project I worked in Python with data from the U.S. Census describing private and public sector spending on construction in the United States from 2002 through 2013 with partial data for 2014. One major objective of this project was to use time series regression methods to analyze and extract meaningful statistics and characteristics surrounding patterns of the two sectors using the SciPy library. Second, matplotlib and its numerical mathematics extension numpy were used to visualize and thus provide a way to remove some of the noise and better understand the trends and patterns presented. The data was mined from this website and which also provides further information about collection methods and sources.
